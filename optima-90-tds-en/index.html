OPTIMA 90Installation, maintenance and document control
INSTALLATION GUIDE

Clean substrate, accurate cuts, correct fixing.

01

Condition

Store profiles in the
installation area before fitting.
Keep them flat and away
from heat sources.

02

Prepare

Substrate should be clean,
dry, stable, dust-free and as
flat as practical along the
wall-floor junction.

03

Cut

Cut profiles with a suitable
mitre saw. Use 45° cuts or
compatible accessories for
neat inside/outside corners.

04

Fix

Use compatible adhesive or
mechanical fixing according
to project conditions. Press
firmly so soft lips close the
gap.

Recommended practice

  • Plan accessories: corners, end caps and connectors
    should match the profile and color.
  • Heat-assisted bending: warm the profile and form it
    to curved walls; a key product advantage.
  • Use neutral cleaning: wipe with a soft cloth and pH-
    neutral cleaner.
  • Test on sample: verify adhesive or cleaning
    agents before large-area application.

Avoid

  • Do not fix to wet, dusty, oily or loose substrates;
    clean and dry the bonding area first.
  • Do not use abrasive pads, aggressive solvents or
    high-alkaline cleaners.
  • Do not expose stored profiles to direct sunlight,
    deformation loads or high heat.

Storage

Store horizontally in original
packaging, protected from
dust, direct sun and excessive
temperature variation.

Maintenance

Routine cleaning requires only
damp wiping. Remove dirt
before it hardens around the
soft edges and joints.

Limitations

Interior finishing profile only. It is
not structural and should not be
used where load-bearing
performance is required.
